Management
Management 108 through 127B are limited to Business Economics and Mathematics/Applied Science majors and Accounting minors during the first enrollment pass. Any junior/senior may enroll on the second pass. Management 200 through 299 (excluding 237), 406, 407, 413A, and 413B may be available to UCLA GRADUATE STUDENTS who meet the requisites for the class, space permitting. Interested students should go to the FIRST CLASS MEETING for instructions and PTE numbers. Management 237A thru 237Q are limited to Master of Financial Engineering (M.F.E.) Program students. Management 402, 403, 405, 408, 409, 410, 411A, 411B, 420, and 430 are limited to M.B.A. and Fully Employed M.B.A. Program students.
Class Notes: Management 108 is limited to Business Economics and Mathematics/Applied Science majors and Accounting minors on the first enrollment pass. Any junior/senior may enroll on the second pass.Other students must obtain a PTE number during the first class meeting.
